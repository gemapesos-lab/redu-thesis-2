# MVL Filipino Sentiment Rating Instructions

Please rate each Filipino or Taglish candidate term using the VADER valence convention:

- `-4` = most negative
- `0` = neutral or no clear sentiment
- `+4` = most positive

Use whole numbers or decimals if needed. Rate the sentiment of the term as it is commonly used in Filipino social-media context. If a term is ambiguous, provide your best rating and add a short note.

These ratings will be screened after collection. A term will be retained only if the three independent expert ratings fall within plus or minus 1 of the mean rating.
